Web and Coding Club
NIT Silchar

Welcome to the house of genius computer geeks and most active club of National Institute of Technology Silchar. We learn coding skills and then practice with some of the most genious brains out there in world on platforms like HackerRank, HackerEarth, CodeChef, SPOJ, etc.- this prepares coders for various international competitions like Google Code Jam and ACM-ICPC. We have coders and developers for nearly all popular languages. Besides this, we also organise various workshops and seminars.
Not only this, the begineers get adequate help from experienced seniors and there is a Computer lab open 24 hours with finest infrastructure dedicated to coding club and yes there is arrangement of snacks, coke and tea-parties too.
